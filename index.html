📋 Notice

GCBD Rules & Regulations — Important Notice for Lot Owners

The Great Canadian Barn Dance (GCBD) campground, adjacent to Mountain Vista Estates, has issued an official Rules & Regulations document governing use of their property and the lake. This applies to all MVE lot owners, visitors, and guests. Key rules include no motorized boats on the lake, flotation devices required, 10 kph speed limit on GCBD property, and all children 15 & under must be accompanied by an adult. Please review the full document and share with your guests.
